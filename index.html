About Our Shop

At VGFL 2 GO, we're passionate about creating a warm and inviting atmosphere where our customers can relax, recharge, and savor the best coffee and tea around. Our journey began with a simple idea: to provide a space where people can come together, share stories, and enjoy a delicious cup of coffee.

Our coffee beans are sourced from the finest coffee-growing regions around the world, and are roasted locally to ensure maximum freshness and flavor. We take great pride in our baristas' expertise, and they're trained to craft the perfect cup every time.

We also offer a wide range of teas, including classic black tea, refreshing green tea, and indulgent herbal blends, all brewed to perfection. And to complement our beverages, we serve a variety of freshly baked pastries and light snacks.

We believe that every cup of coffee or tea is a moment to be savored, which is why we strive to make each visit to our shop a special one. So whether you're a coffee connoisseur, a tea lover, or simply looking for a cozy and welcoming place to relax, we invite you to come visit us and experience the warmth and charm of our coffee shop.
